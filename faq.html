Employer Questions

ACES helps employers obtain the benefits of hiring persons experiencing disability, including having a diverse, stable and productive workforce.

Benefits to the Employer
Findings show that benefits of hiring people with disabilities included improvements in profitability (e.g., profits and cost-effectiveness, turnover and retention, reliability and punctuality, employee loyalty, company image), competitive advantage (e.g., diverse customers, customer loyalty and satisfaction, innovation, productivity, work ethic, safety).
Tax Benefits?
Businesses who hire individuals with disabilities may be eligible for sizable federal and state tax credits. One in particular is the Work Opportunity Credit that provides eligible employers with a tax credit on the first $6,000 of first-year wages of a new employee with a disability. These figures may change and if you wish to know more please reach out to ACES now.
