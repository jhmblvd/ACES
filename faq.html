FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions (5)

Information and requirements change. There is a lot of information to get through. ACES will gladly help you navigate the ocean of information to find you the program that will help you succeed.

Do you help people on Social Security?
Yes! We help many people who are on Social Security. There are many programs available.
Will I lose my Social Security check or my benefits while working or training?
You can work and retain your Social Security as well as your benefits. Contact us for now for an individual assessment!
Do you work with Unemployement?
Yes!
Do you work with Vocational Rehab?
Yes we often work with Vocational Rehabilitation finding our clients meaningful employment.
What kind of programs do you offer?
There are many programs available that will help you find work. Our goal is to meet you where you are Discover your strengths and weaknesses, and tailor a program to fit your needs.
Do you work with employers?
We do. If you are an employer seeking help please contact us today. We can help you setup a program and inform you of the many advantages available to employers today.
