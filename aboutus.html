Who we are

ABOUT ACES

Daryl Ackley

Ackley Counseling & Employment Services (ACES) was created 10 years ago, after my retirement from the State of Oregon as a vocational rehabilitation counselor for the Oregon Commission for the Blind (OCB). I wanted to continue to help people experiencing disability to achieve their highest potential through employment or self-employment, with a focus on working with folks experiencing intellectual/developmental disabilities (I/DD).

When I started ACES I contracted with Vocational Rehabilitation, OCB and Oregon Developmental Disabilities Services (ODDS) as a job developer in several counties in Southern Oregon. I expanded the business to become a fully certified Medicaid I/DD provider organization and we have grown to nearly 15 employees in 3 counties, providing a wide range of services. Our vision and commitment is to provide world-class diamond quality services and help people achieve their vocational and independent living goals.

DISCOVER

The Process

Discovery is a specialized type of career exploration provided for up to 90 days through referral from an individual’s Personal Agent or Service Coordinator at their Community Developmental Disabilities program. During Discovery we focus on “finding a person’s unique personal genius” as a method to help a person determine a career or job goal they will enjoy and be successful in. Discovery includes working with a team to complete an interactive vocational futures plan, visiting employers for tours and informational interviews on jobs. At the end of Discovery we will have an idea of a job direction, support needs, and a person’s skills, abilities, likes and successes.

EXPLORE

DEVELOP

After discoverying areas most suitable for our participants we begin to develop a plan. The plan may involve many processes with the end result of developing a strategy that helps you find the job that will bring the most reward and have the highest chance of bringing you forward. It may involve contracted services through our partnership with Vocational Rehabilitation Services and/or Oregon Commission for the Blind.

APPLY

Real World Use

From discovery through employment we work with participants and partners to develop a strategy we then apply that strategy in finding participants meaningful employment, with continued support that will help them continue in that work and succeed long term.
