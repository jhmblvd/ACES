Employment and Independent Living Services For People Experiencing Intellectual/Developmental Disabilities.

ACES provides a wide range of services to individuals experiencing intellectual/developmental disabilities. Ackley Counseling & Employment Services (ACES) has been serving the community and working with community partners for 12 years. For a full list of our services click the button below!

Our Mission

ACES mission is to help people achieve their highest potential through employment. Our values are open & honest communication, outcome based joyful action, community service, compassionate service, & diamond quality world-class service.

Core Values

ACES helps people who experience intellectual/developmental disabilities succeed in achieving their highest potential through employment and independent living.

ACES helps employers expand to a diverse and financially productive workforce by assisting them in hiring people experiencing intellectual/developmental disabilities.
