Providing a wide range of services to individuals with disabilities. Ackley Counseling and Employment Services (ACES) has been serving the community and working with community partners for 10 years. For a full list of our services click the button below!

Our Services

Serving participants for 10 Years!

ACES is located in Medford Oregon, we also have offices in Klamath Falls and Grants Pass. Our goal is to empower people through meaningful employment which is employment that provides support, meaning, and purpose, to our participant's lives

Finding options for success

We believe meaningful employment can can provide a person with options to achieve the goals they set, empowering the individual with a sense of self sufficiency and pride.

One on One Counseling

In the quest to provide meaningful employment for our participants we provide employment support throughout the job finding process. This process includes evidence based steps helping to find the right fit for each participant.

Social Security and Benefits Planning

Providing key support in Social Security Benefits Planning is important for many of our participants and at ACES we work hard finding the key supports our participants need to be successful in the workplace.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people achieve their highest potential through employment. Our values are open & honest communication, outcome based joyful action, community service, compassionate service, & diamond quality world-class service."
